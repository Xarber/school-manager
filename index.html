Classes

Schedule your activities and manage your school schedule!

What do I need this for?

Classes was designed for school activities, scheduled exams and managing your homework.
You may, however, adapt it for any other use such as scheduling events or preparing surveys!
It supports role management so both students and teachers can use it.
You can join multiple classes to manage different activities and/or groups.

How will I manage activities?

You will be provided with a common calendar with all your group schedulings.
You will be notified before an event, or when a new survey/scheduled exam is available.
You can install the app on your home screen for easy access.
System calendar integration is available, you can add a calendar for each class.

How much does it cost?

Classes is free to use, but you can support the development by sending bug reports or feature suggestions to xarber616@gmail.com
Classes is not open source, to protect user data from eventual hacks.

What about data management?

Classes does provides basic data encryption as of now. Regardless, avoid storing sensitive data in it.
Data is stored on the X-Center server; you can request data deletion at any time on the website itself.
